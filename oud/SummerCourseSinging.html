SUMMER COURSE SINGING
by PIERRE MAK & DAVID BOLLEN
HUNGARY 21 JULY – 1 AUGUST 2009



 

After having given summer courses together for years in the Netherlands, Pierre Mak and David Bollen decided to organize the 2009 course in Hungary. Getting away from the everyday life doesn’t only offer the students great vacation included, but also enhances the learning conditions: you don’t have anything else to do but to concentrate on singing.


During the 2-week course each participant will have 5 lessons with voice teacher Pierre Mak, 5 with vocal coach David Bollen and 1 with the two teachers together. One hour lesson per day leaves you with enough time to practice and listen to each other’s lessons. Furthermore, you will have the opportunity to perform at the students’ concerts during the course. The lessons will be given in a school. There are enough rooms with pianos for self-study.
You will be staying all in one house in 2 and 3 bedroom apartments. There is a kitchen with possibility for cooking. For those who prefer eating out, there are plenty of restaurants nearby varying from inexpensive to chique.
The location, Balatonfüred, is a charming town at the shore of Hungary’s number one summer resort, Lake Balaton. Leisure activities include swimming, sailing, windsurfing, horseback riding, wine tasting or visiting Balaton’s most picturesque part, the Tihany Peninsula.

Participation fee: € 850,-

The price includes:

  • 5 lessons of 60 minutes with Pierre Mak
  • 5 lessons of 60 minutes with David Bollen
  • 1 lesson of 60 minutes with both teachers

Accommodation in 2 and 3 bedroom apartments, 13 nights.
