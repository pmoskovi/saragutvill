Booking

You can book the following recitals of Sára’s:

  • Händel: 9 German Aria’s (with baroque ensemble)
  • Brahmzzzz! Recital with songs of Johannes Brahms a.o. Zigeunerlieder and Zwei Gesänge (with piano and viola)
  • Frauen, Liebe und Leben: Songs of Clara en Robert Schumann (with piano)
  • Kholem: Yiddish Songs (with accordeon and violin)
  • Di Alte Kashe: Yiddish Songs arranged by Viktor Ullmann, Darius Milhaud, Dmitri Shostakovich, Maurice Ravel and Iván Fischer (met piano en trumpet)
  • FolkSong: arrangement of folk songs by different composers, a.o. Bartók, Brahms, Dvořák, Kodály and Janáček (with piano)

Besides her recitals Sára arranges programs for different kinds of occasions. In 2006 (Heine-year) she put together a program of songs on poems by Heinrich Heine (on request of the German Library in The Hague). In 2007 she sang a recital on the theme of Max Beckmann on the occasion of an exhibition of his works in the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam. Besides these thematically composed programs Sára often compiles programs for personal occasions like weddings, anniversaries and funerals.
