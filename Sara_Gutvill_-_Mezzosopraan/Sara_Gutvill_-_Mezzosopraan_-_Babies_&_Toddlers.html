Sing for me!
For babies, toddlers and their parents


Children are by nature music lovers. Every child has a natural musicality, enjoys making all kinds of sounds and listening to music.

De course is based on the interaction between parent and child with music as medium. We sing and play together and let the children experiment with simple instruments. This is above all fun but at the same time stimulates your child’s motor and language skills.

There are two groups, one for babies up to two years and one for toddlers from two to four years. We use a wide array of songs in different styles and languages according to the background of the participants. You can take the songs home with you on a sheet. The course is also a nice opportunity for the children to make friends and for the parents to share their experience. The course starts in January and consists of 12 sessions of 45 minutes. Main language: English.
