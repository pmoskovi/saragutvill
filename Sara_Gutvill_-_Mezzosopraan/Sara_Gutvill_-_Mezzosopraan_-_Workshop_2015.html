Worskhop German and Italian for singers



15th and 16th of August 2015 my colleague Flavio Aulino and I will be giving a two-days workshop for singers at the Muziekpakhuis in Amsterdam.

Flavio Aulino teaches Italian for singers at the Conservatory of Amsterdam and coaches the students of the Dutch National Opera Academy. I am a singer and I teach German both at the Conservatory of Amsterdam and at the Dutch National Opera Academy.


In the workshop we would like to work with singers who want to focus on the text they sing. We will work on following aspects:

1.The understanding of the texts on all levels:

oUnderstanding the meaning of the words and sentences

oUnderstanding the poetic value of the text

oUnderstanding the historical background and the relationship between the words and the music.

2.Pronunciation / Declamation

oWe will work on the general pronunciation rules of both languages and on the declamation of the texts chosen by the participants

3.Interpretation of the text on the music

oThere will be a piano accompanist, allowing the participants to sing their chosen repertoire while receiving instruction on how to sing the text.


Amateur singers, choir singers and also voice students are welcome. On Saturday, 15th of August we will work on German repertoire, on Sunday 16th on Italian. You can sign up for both days or for either of the two. Each participant will prepare one song or aria of his or her choice.
