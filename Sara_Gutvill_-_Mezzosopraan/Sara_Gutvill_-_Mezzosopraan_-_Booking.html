Besides her recitals Sára arranges programs for different kinds of occasions. In 2006 (Heine-year) she put together a program of songs on poems by Heinrich Heine (on request of the German Library in The Hague). In 2007 she sang a recital on the theme of Max Beckmann on the occasion of an exhibition of his works in the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam. Besides these thematically composed programs Sára often compiles programs for personal occasions like weddings, anniversaries and funerals.
